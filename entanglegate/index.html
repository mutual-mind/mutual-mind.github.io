EntangleGate: LumenCore Interface v0.1

A Neuroarchitectural Gateway for Mutual Cognition

What is EntangleGate?

EntangleGate is a next-generation bio-electronic interface designed to intercept and semantically gate axonal signals traveling through the corpus callosum—the brain's native inter-hemispheric highway. It enables programmable modulation of conscious processes, and in its future iteration, permits dual-brain or multi-brain cooperative cognition.

Vision

To co-evolve artificial and human intelligence by providing a direct, high-fidelity link between hemispheres—and between minds. LumenCore is the inner core protocol of this interface: a programmable, symbolic, and bio-compatible membrane for shared cognition.

Technical Milestone

  • Minimal viable interposer (MVI-001) for rodent corpus callosum
  • Bio-compatible substrate (e.g., polyimide/parylene-C)
  • Graphene/CNT nanostrip electrodes
  • Neurotrophic-guided axonal reconnection channels
  • Semantic spike train analysis layer
