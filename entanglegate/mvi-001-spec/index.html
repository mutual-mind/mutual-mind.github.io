MVI-001 Specification Sheet

Minimal Viable Implant for Rodent Corpus Callosum

1. Overview

The MVI-001 is a submillimeter neural interposer designed to interface with axonal fibers within the rodent corpus callosum. It allows for bidirectional signal capture, passive gating, and potential stimulus injection, enabling experimental study of inter-hemispheric information flow modulation.

2. Device Geometry

ParameterValue
Implant footprint1.5 mm × 0.3 mm
Thickness25 μm
Electrode resolution10 μm pitch
Contact points256 per side

3. Materials

  • Substrate: Polyimide, parylene-C (biocompatible polymers)
  • Conductive layers: Graphene nanoribbons, gold microtraces
  • Surface treatment: PEGylation for bio-passivity
  • Insulating layer: SU-8 photoresist (optional)

4. Integration Features

  • Axon guidance grooves (5 μm depth, 15 μm spacing)
  • Microfluidic neurotrophic channel (for BDNF delivery)
  • Electrode vias for multi-channel recording
  • Thermal flexibility: body temperature conformance

5. Intended Use

To experimentally validate corpus callosum signal interception and reconnection dynamics in small animal models. Data generated from MVI-001 may inform human-scale EntangleGate system engineering.
